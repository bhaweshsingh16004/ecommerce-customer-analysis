E-commerce Customer Analytics (SQL + Python)

 Objective
Analyze customer transactions to generate business insights and support decision-making.

Key Analysis
- Total revenue calculation
- Monthly sales trends
- Top customers identification
- Customer segmentation

 Tools Used
- MySQL
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)

Key Insights
•	Identified high-value customers contributing most revenue
•	Observed trends in monthly sales performance
•	Analyzed product category impact
•	Revenue shows variation across months, indicating potential seasonal trends 
•	Certain product categories contribute more to overall revenue 
•	A small group of customers generates a large portion of total sales 

 Business Impact
This project demonstrates how data analysis can help businesses optimize marketing strategies, improve customer targeting, and increase revenue.

💡 Business Recommendations
•	Focus marketing campaigns on high-value customers 
•	Increase promotions during low-revenue periods 
•	Optimize inventory for top-performing product categories


 Conclusion
Combining SQL and Python enables efficient data analysis and visualization for real-world business problems.
